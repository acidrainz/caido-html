Our Philosophy



CAIDAO 《財道》is an extract of a proverb meaning “The Road to Prosperity”. The proverb from which it originates captures the spirit of what we represent: Strategic, Financial, Wisdom. This philosophy begins within our team, is reflected in our products, and extends to our partners and investors.


The Chinese proverb Sheng Cai You Dao “生財有道” emphasizes both physical and spiritual well-being at the highest ethical standards while on a path of fortune or wealth. Sheng Cai translates into “generating wealth in high spirits and bursting with life" and Dao simply means "road“. Dao is the same word as in Daoism (Taoism), which represents the balance of yin and yang, fortune and a life eternal. It is also translated as “The Way”.

In 1997, she joined MeesPierson and oversaw their Asian fixed income portfolios and restructured the front-middle-back office throughout the Asian financial crisis. Furthermore, she was in charge of an USD800M portfolio. Ming also held a senior position with Barclays before founding a boutique alternative asset investment consulting firm advising families, institutional investors and fund managers on a variety of strategic initiatives and business operational improvements.

Oppenheim operations in Asia and established the institutional processes and procedures, including, but not limited to, due diligence, manager selection, portfolio construction, risk management and compliance for hedge fund and private equity portfolios. Following the takeover by Deutsche Bank AG, a management buy-out was completed and CAIDAO CAPITAL Limited was born.

    She is licensed to conduct Type 4 (Advising on Securities) and Type 9 (Asset Management) Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission regulated activities.

    Ming holds a dual BA Cum Laude degree from the University of Maryland in Economics and in Chinese Language & Literature. She is also a Trustee of Asian Art Museum Foundation of the Asian Art Museum of San Francisco. She also sits on various boards of their investee companies.
