Our Philosophy



CAIDAO 《財道》is an extract of a proverb meaning “The Road to Prosperity”.The proverb from which it originates captures the spirit of what we represent: Strategic, Financial, Wisdom. This philosophy begins within our team, is reflected in our products, and extends to our partners and investors.


The Chinese proverb Sheng Cai You Dao “生財有道” emphasizes both physical and spiritual well-being at the highest ethical standards while on apath of fortune or wealth. Sheng Cai translates into “generating wealth in high spirits and bursting with life" and Dao simply means "road“. Dao is the same word as in Daoism (Taoism), which represents the balance of yin and yang, fortune and a life eternal. It is also translated as “The Way”.

meet OUR TEAM

  • OUR Founder

    Ming Lee


    Ming LEE is CAIDAO | CAPITAL’s Founder and Chief Executive Officer. She has over 30 years of global financial services experiences in private banking, alternative investment management, and consulting firms. She has a proven track record building and restructuring businesses in both entrepreneurial and corporate environments.

    In 1986, Ming worked with a specialized consulting firm in Beijing and helped multi-national companies establish government and business relationships in China. She also worked with one of the large import/export corporations in China to help them revitalize underproductive factories.

    She subsequently joined Citibank’s Private Bank and underwrote extensive commercial real estate properties in Northern California. Over time, she was responsible for managing a portfolio of approximately USD450M in the 1990’s and went on to re-engineer the jumbo mortgage program in the U.S.

    Ming returned to Asia in 1995 and worked with ultra-high net worth families in Hong Kong and the Philippines. In 1997, she joined MeesPierson and oversaw their Asian fixed income portfolios and restructured the front-middle-back office throughout the Asian financial crisis. Furthermore, she was in charge of an USD800M portfolio. Ming also held a senior position with Barclays before founding a boutique alternative asset investment consulting firm advising families, institutional investors and fund managers on a variety of strategic initiatives and business operational improvements.

    In 2007, she set up the Sal. Oppenheim operations in Asia and established the institutional processes and procedures, including, but not limited to, due diligence, manager selection, portfolio construction, risk management and compliance for hedge fund and private equity portfolios. Following the takeover by Deutsche Bank AG, a management buy-out was completed and CAIDAO CAPITAL Limited was born.

    She is licensed to conduct Type 4 (Advising on Securities) and Type 9 (Asset Management) Hong Kong Securities and Futures Commission regulated activities.

    Ming holds a dual BA Cum Laude degree from the University of Maryland in Economics and in Chinese Language & Literature. She is also a Trustee of Asian Art Museum Foundation of the Asian Art Museum of San Francisco. She also sits on various boards of their investee companies.
